About Me

The Design Process

I know the circumstances under which I design best, but the actual process often feels much like this:

  • Coffeefirst.
  • Digitalpainting/layout.
  • Magichappens.
  • Designis created with
  • Codein mind.

Truthfully, this is a process that I love - Design and creation are in my blood. My work ranges from print/digital advertisements all the way to tattoo designs. Most recently I was working in the Specialty Coffee Industry as a marketer wherein I was able to work with (and make) lots of gorgeous imagery. I have also created t-shirt designs, story illustrations, and website layouts. Currently, I'm learning to code and this site is a testament to that work. HTML, CSS, and Javascript are where I'm focusing my efforts.
